# AI Session Logs

> This folder is **required**. Submissions without it are incomplete.

---

## What To Include

We want to see how you work with AI, not just what you produced.

Include **at least one of the following per major decision or implementation task:**

- **Screenshots** of your AI sessions (ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Copilot, etc.)
- **Exported chat logs** (markdown, PDF, or raw text)
- **Your own annotated summaries** of what you prompted, what the AI returned, and what you did with it

---

## What We're Evaluating

| Signal | What it tells us |
|---|---|
| Prompt structure | Can they communicate intent clearly to an AI? |
| Iteration | Did they accept the first response or refine? |
| Steering | Did they catch errors and correct course? |
| Rejection | Did they push back on bad suggestions? |
| Validation | How did they verify AI output was correct? |

---

## Naming Convention

Name your files clearly:

```
01-initial-architecture-brainstorm.md
02-prompt-design-for-categorisation.png
03-hallucination-example-and-fix.png
04-test-strategy-session.md
```

---

## Minimum Bar

At least **3 session logs** covering:

1. An architecture or design decision
2. Your AI prompt design for the categorisation feature
3. At least one moment where the AI got something wrong and you corrected it

---

## Annotate Your Logs

Don't just paste logs. Add commentary:

```
## What I was trying to do
[Context]

## What I prompted
[Your prompt]

## What AI returned
[Response or screenshot]

## What I noticed
[What was right, wrong, or surprising]

## What I did next
[How you steered or acted on it]
```
